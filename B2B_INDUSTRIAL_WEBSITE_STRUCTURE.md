## CONTENT GUIDELINES FOLLOWED

### ✅ Factual Language
- No invented services, machines, or certifications
- Only mentioned: ISO 9001:2015, Hosur India, OEM customers
- Specific processes: CNC machining, forging, sheet metal
- Real capabilities: In-house design, DFM, prototyping

### ✅ No Marketing Buzzwords
- Avoided: "innovative", "best", "leading", "world-class", "cutting-edge"
- Used: "precision", "certified", "specializing", "consistent", "disciplined"

### ✅ Industrial, OEM-Focused Tone
- Technical language appropriate for B2B
- Focus on quality, reliability, cost-effectiveness
- Emphasis on long-term partnerships
- Process-oriented descriptions

### ✅ SEO Optimization (Natural)
- Keywords integrated naturally
- No keyword stuffing
- Clear, descriptive headings
- Industry-specific terminology
